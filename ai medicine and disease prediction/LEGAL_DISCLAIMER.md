# Legal & Product Disclaimer

## AI-Powered Clinical Decision Support & Prescription Fulfillment Platform

### Product Positioning

This system is **NOT** a direct medicine prescription system. It is designed as:

- **Clinical Decision Support** – Assists healthcare decision-making with AI-powered risk assessment
- **Prescription Fulfillment** – Enables medicine ordering only from valid doctor prescriptions
- **Health Guidance** – Provides educational content and lifestyle recommendations

### Core Principles

1. **No Direct Prescriptions** – The AI never prescribes medicines. It only recommends consulting a doctor.
2. **Symptom-to-Risk Analysis** – Predicts possible disease risk from symptoms with probability scores.
3. **Prescription-Required Flow** – Medicines can only be ordered when a valid doctor prescription is uploaded and verified.
4. **Educational Purpose** – Disease information, diet, exercise, and lifestyle advice are for education only.

### Legal Disclaimer (Display Everywhere)

> **This system does not replace professional medical advice.**
> Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.
> In case of medical emergency, seek immediate medical care or call emergency services.

### Ethical AI Documentation

- **Explainability**: Predictions include feature importance and human-readable reasoning.
- **Transparency**: Confidence scores and urgency levels are clearly displayed.
- **Privacy**: User health data is handled with appropriate security measures.
- **Bias Mitigation**: Model evaluation metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, F1) are tracked.

### Intended Use

- Academic/research demonstration
- Clinical decision support augmentation
- Prescription workflow management
- Health tracking and wellness guidance

### Not Intended For

- Replacing licensed medical professionals
- Self-diagnosis without doctor consultation
- Purchasing prescription medicines without valid prescription
